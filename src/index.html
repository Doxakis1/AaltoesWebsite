ABOUT US

Aalto Entrepreneurship Society (Aaltoes) is the most prominent and most active student-run entrepreneurship society in Europe. Our main goal is simple – build the next generation of entrepreneurs. Over the years we’ve done this by building events and programs targeted from the new university student to those who are already running their startup.

We organise events that inspire students to show role models of successful founders, and to keep striving and reaching for bigger goals and showing that tremendous success comes only by working hard and smart. Alongside inspiration, events provide knowledge on how to build startups.

We believe that the best way to learn entrepreneurial skills is to start building something of your own, either as a volunteer building a project or building your own company. Our community consists of highly ambitious and driven people who push themselves and the others forward, chase the limits and give back to the community. We are building the world’s next big things, it’s up to you if you want to be in the audience or the leader of change.

Currently the Aaltoes team is building 5 bigger projects: FR8, Strive, Ignite, Fusion & a startup idea validation course.

HISTORY

In 2008 a university professor in Helsinki told his students that becoming an entrepreneur is the worst decision you can make. At that time, the Finnish mindset wasn’t an entrepreneurial one – taking risks or giving a shot at your idea wasn’t respected. A group of students from Aalto University decided that it was time for a paradigm shift and embarked on a trip that would shape not only Aalto University but the whole of Finland...
